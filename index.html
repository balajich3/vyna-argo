About Us

At VYNA AGRO, we produce 100% organic jaggery using traditional methods without any additives or chemicals. Our jaggery is made from high-quality sugarcane grown without synthetic pesticides or GMOs. We're proud to be FSSAI and Organic India certified, along with many other global standards.

Our Mission & Vision

To promote organic farming practices while delivering the highest quality jaggery to consumers. We envision a healthy future with natural alternatives to refined sugar.

Our Products

Solid Block Jaggery

Traditional jaggery blocks made from pure sugarcane juice, perfect for cooking and health benefits.

Powdered Jaggery

Convenient to use in tea, coffee, sweets, and daily consumption – all organic and unrefined.

Liquid Jaggery

Perfect as a sweetener in beverages and desserts. Easy to mix, delicious to taste.

Specialty Jaggery

Includes flavored, herbal, or spiced jaggery – healthy and tasty for every household.
